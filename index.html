Stega PNG

Hide secret messages
in image files.

This demo uses steganography to embed secret data into an image.

The secret message is encoded into the least-significant bits of the image's pixel data, making it invisible to the naked eye and difficult to detect.

None of your data are transmitted over the network. All operations are performed locally on your web browser.
